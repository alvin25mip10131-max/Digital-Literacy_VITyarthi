## Part B – Social Media Do's and Don'ts  ##

✅ Do’s
-------------------------------------
 1) Use strong and unique passwords.
 2) Think before posting any content.
 3) Maintain privacy settings.
 4) Share accurate and reliable information.
 5) Communicate respectfully and professionally.

 ❌ Don’ts
 -----------------------------------
  1) Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
  2) Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
  3) Do not click on suspicious links or messages.
  4) Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying.
  5) Do not overshare your location or daily activities.
