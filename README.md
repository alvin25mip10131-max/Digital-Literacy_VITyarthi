##  Module Summaries

###  Module 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
In this module, I created an infographic to explain the concept of digital literacy and its importance in today’s world. I focused on making the content visually engaging and easy to understand. The infographic covers topics such as safe internet practices, responsible social media usage, digital footprints, and cybersecurity awareness. This activity improved my creativity and helped me learn how to present complex ideas in a simple and clear way.

---

### Module 2: Digital Portfolio
In this module, I developed my professional online presence using platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn. I updated my profiles by adding academic details, skills, and project work. I also learned how to structure a portfolio effectively so that it is attractive and informative. This module helped me understand the importance of maintaining a strong digital identity for future opportunities.

---

###  Module 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
In this module, I explored platforms like HackerRank and Google Forms. I practiced coding problems to improve my logical thinking and problem-solving skills. I also created a digital literacy quiz using Google Forms, which involved designing questions and sharing them with others. This helped me understand collaborative tools and the importance of continuous learning.

---

###  Module 4: Email Etiquette & Communication
In this module, I learned how to communicate professionally in a digital environment. I practiced writing formal emails with proper structure, tone, and clarity. I also created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts. This module helped me improve my communication skills and understand how to present myself professionally online.

---

###  Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness
In this module, I studied real-world cybercrime cases such as phishing, identity theft, hacking, and online fraud. I also created a checklist of preventive measures like using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links. This module increased my awareness of cybersecurity and the importance of staying safe online.
