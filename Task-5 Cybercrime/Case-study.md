## Case Study: MobiKwik UPI Payment Fraud (2025) ##
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Crime Type: Online Payment Fraud / UPI Fraud
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Description
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   In September 2025 the Indian company MobiKwik had a problem with online payment fraud. This was one of the digital payment frauds in the countrys history. The people who did this found a point in MobiKwiks payment system after they updated their software. Over two days on September 11 and 12 these people made transactions that added up to around Rs 40 crore. They took money from MobiKwiks accounts.

Step-by-Step Execution
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  1) They found a weakness in the system.
    MobiKwik had a problem with their system that let people make UPI transactions even if they did not have money in their wallets.

  2) They got around the security.
    The system let people use UPI PINs, which is a big security mistake.

  3) No one noticed what was happening.
     These fake transactions were not seen for two days until someone checked on September 13.

  4) Some people were arrested.
     The police caught six people. They think that someone who works at MobiKwik might have helped them because the attack was very clever.

Who was. What happened to them
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
MobiKwik was the main victim but over 5 lakh people were also affected by these fake transactions. Some bad things that happened were:

  .They lost money. Rs 40 crore was taken and only Rs 14 crore was gotten back

  .The government looked at them. They found out that MobiKwik was not good at stopping fraud

  .People did not trust MobiKwik anymore. This was the time they had a big security problem

This shows that if you do not watch what is going on and someone on the inside is helping you can have problems, with online payment fraud.
